About Bikana Bites

Bikana Bites brings the authentic taste of Bikaner to snack lovers across India. Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality namkeen products that combine traditional recipes with modern packaging and hygiene standards.


Every pack of Bikana Bites is prepared with carefully selected ingredients, ensuring freshness, crunch and flavour in every bite. From classic bhujia to delicious moong dal, our products are crafted to satisfy every namkeen lover.


We believe great taste should be affordable. That is why our products are available at pocket-friendly prices while maintaining premium quality.


As we continue to grow, our goal is to become one of India's most trusted snack brands and build a strong distribution network throughout the country.
